Info

Pattanchery has a total of 4322 families residing. The Pattanchery village has population of 18712 of which 9262 are males while 9450 are females as per Population Census 2011.

Train Station

Nearest Train Station is Kollengode and Pudunagaram Railway station. Main is Olavakkode Railway Station(Palghat) which is 20+ kms from Pattanchery
