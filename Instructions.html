INSTRUCTIONS

Guidance for Digital Process and Commercial Things to do

Electricity Bill Online Payment

Go to this website : https://wss.kseb.in and You can either keep registered or you can pay using quick payment. Android App Link.

Aadhar Details Update Online

Update Aadhar Details online for free of cost using this website : https://uidai.gov.in. Name, Address, DoB, Gender, Mobile & Email can be updated online.

Passport Application

Use this website : https://portal2.passportindia.gov.in to Apply Passport online. Upload Relevant documents and Verification schedulings.

Digilocker

E-sign your Govt approved documents and Use it for displaying to Officials at emergency times : https://digilocker.gov.in/ (License, Aadhar Card, etc) - Android App Link

Travel Ticket Bookings

Use this link : https://www.irctc.co.in/ to Book Train and Travel tickets using Online Payment Methods. (Government Approved Website)

e-District (Akshaya Centre)

Use this website : https://edistrict.kerala.gov.in for Forms Download for every digital service available. Official Website portal.

Online Shopping

Websites for Online shopping : Amazon, Flipkart, E-Bay, PayTM. Get your things delivered at your doorstep.

Find Vehicle Owner Details

Use this website : https://parivahan.gov.in to Find Owner Name, RC and DL details using Vehicle Registration Number.

Kerala e-Services

Offical Web Portal of Kerala Government : https://www.kerala.gov.in/. Contains Certificates, Utility Payments, Information about Kerala services.

Apply License Online

Apply Learners License online, Renewal, Duplicate License, Registration and many more are dealt in this website : https://mvd.kerala.gov.in/

BHIM UPI Transfers

Use Seamless transactions using Mobile number linked with your bank account.(Google Pay, PhonePe) BHIM App Link

PAN Card Online

Update your PAN Card details or Take printout of your digital PAN Card online using this link : https://www.onlineservices.nsdl.com

Money Transfer Online

Use online bank websites for money transfer. Commision tax is applied but reduces the efforts of standing in queue and Paperfree. (SBI, SIB and many more..)

 

Online Mobile Recharge

Search for Online Subscriber websites like Jio, Airtel, BSNL, Vodafone and Recharge online using different payment methods.
